Open-source contributions to Hugging Face Transformers, with emphasis on computer vision and multimodal model support.

Highlights:

* Led the addition of Segment Anything 2 (SAM2) support, including model integration, image/video processors, conversion flow, documentation, and integration tests.
* Opened Molmo2 support and published `danelcsb/Molmo2-4B` on the Hugging Face Hub.
* Implemented or improved RT-DETR, ViTPose, DETA training loss, and distributed training utilities.
